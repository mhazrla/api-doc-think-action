# Weekly Goal

As the name suggests, weekly Goal are a core part of Protocol — the very reason Protocol exists is so you can have secure conversations with your weekly Goal. On this page, we'll dive into the different weekly goal endpoints you can use to manage weekly Goal programmatically. We'll look at how to query, create, update, and delete weekly Goal.

## The Weekly Goal model

The weekly Goal model contains all the information about your weekly Goal, such as their username, avatar, and username. It also contains a reference to the conversation between you and the weekly goal and information about when they were last active on Protocol.

### Properties

<Properties>
  <Property name="_id" type="string">
    Unique identifier for the weekly Goal.
  </Property>
  <Property name="weekly_goal" type="string">
    The weekly_goal for the weekly Goal.
  </Property>
  <Property name="photo" type="string">
    The photo for the weekly Goal.
  </Property>
  <Property name="due_date" type="string">
    The weekly Goal due_date in the weekly Goal list.
  </Property>
  <Property name="share_with" type="string">
    The weekly Goal share_with in the weekly Goal list.
  </Property>
  <Property name="reminder" type="string">
    The weekly Goal reminder in the weekly Goal list.
  </Property>
  <Property name="yearly_id" type="string">
    The weekly Goal yearly_id in the weekly Goal list.
  </Property>
  <Property name="user_id" type="boolean">
    The weekly goal accout in the weekly Goal list.
  </Property>
</Properties>

      <Property name="yearly_id" type="string">

      <Property name="user_id" type="string">

  </Col>
